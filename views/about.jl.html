About Me

Welcome to the GasChromatographyToolbox. I'm Jan Leppert, a passionate developer specializing in scientific software development and data analysis. This web application showcases my expertise in creating tools for analytical chemistry, specifically focusing on gas chromatographic separations.

With a background in both software development and analytical chemistry, I bring a unique perspective to scientific software solutions. My approach combines robust mathematical modeling with intuitive user interfaces, making complex analytical processes more accessible.

This toolbox represents my commitment to open science and practical solutions. It enables chemists and researchers to simulate and optimize their gas chromatographic separations, saving valuable time and resources in the laboratory.

As a freelancer, I'm always open to new challenges and collaborations. Whether you need custom scientific software development, data analysis solutions, or consulting on your analytical chemistry projects, I'm here to help turn your ideas into reality.
